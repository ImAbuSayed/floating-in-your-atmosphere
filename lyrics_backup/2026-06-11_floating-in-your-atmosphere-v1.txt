[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
[Breathy]
I lose my gravity when you pull me near
Caught in a quiet pull that makes it so clear
Shadows are falling and the world disappears
I am just floating in your atmosphere

[Verse 1]
We left the headlights out on the gravel drive
Just the porch embers keeping the night alive
Your fingers tracing a map across my skin
This is the only place I want to be in

[Pre-Chorus]
Every breath you take is an ocean tide
Pulling me deeper, nowhere to hide
I drop my defenses, I give you the reins
Electricity racing right through my veins

[Chorus]
[Soulful]
I am floating in your atmosphere
Every soft whisper is all that I hear
The rest of the room just fades into dust
Caught in the gravity, wrapped in your trust

[Echo] (Wrapped in your trust)
